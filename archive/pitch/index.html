GT100K01 / 08
Context

GT100K is the learning OS for GT school, layered on TimeBack.

  • ›Two-hour focused core. Mastery-based practice where 1 minute = 1 XP; 120 XP unlocks the day's work — completed as 4 × 30-minute sections.
  • ›Then project-based time. Once the core is met, learners move into applied, self-directed projects.
  • ›GT dial-up. For the gifted program we raise the focused core to 3–4 hours/day — more depth, same guardrails.
A learner's day
Focused core (XP-gated mastery) → project-based time. GT extends the core to 3–4 hrs. Illustrative structure, not a schedule.

GT100K sits above TimeBack as the software layer that decides what to practice, how help is given, and how progress is proven. The three proposals below all live in that layer.

GT100K03 / 08
Idea 01 · Tutoring

Answer-Blind Socratic Tutor + potential-based independence reward

Problem

Powerful AI in a child's hands invites answer-copying and learned dependence — the exact failure mode a gifted program can't afford.

Our mechanism

The tutor never sees or generates the answer — a separate grader owns answer keys in a different trust domain. It's disabled during mastery quizzes and requires an attempt before a hint. Reward is potential-based: you score the mastery-gain from your own unassisted work.

Why it's safe & defensible

It structurally cannot create AI dependence, and it never punishes a child for asking for help — help never lowers access, credit, or standing. An attempt right after an AI rescue earns ~0.

Evidence

Grounded in established learning science and reward theory, with adversarial testing.

Bjork — desirable difficulties Kapur — productive failure Ng, Harada & Russell 1999 — potential-based shaping Red-team: answer-leakage <1%
GT100K04 / 08
Idea 02 · Credentialing

EvidenceGraph — proof-of-process, not cheating-detection

Problem

In an AI world, "did the student really do this?" is unanswerable — and naive AI cheating-detectors falsely accuse real students, which is reputationally catastrophic.

Our mechanism

Every artifact, attempt, revision, and AI-assist becomes a node in a content-addressed provenance graph (SHA-256/BLAKE3 DAG + in-toto attestation + append-only transparency log). Humans issue every final grade; a model output is only cited supporting evidence, never an accusation. Erasure via crypto-shredding.

Why it's safe & defensible

Credential claims are cryptographically backed and independently verifiable. The system never auto-accuses a child of AI-authorship — it documents process, it doesn't police it. COPPA/FERPA-aligned by design.

Evidence

Built on mature open provenance and supply-chain standards.

W3C PROV + Workflow-Run RO-Crate SLSA / in-toto / transparency log C2PA — export-only, limits acknowledged
GT100K05 / 08
Idea 03 · Motivation

Interest Lab + mutable InterestHypothesis

Problem

Test scores don't capture what actually makes elite performers: durable passion. And crude "talent profiling" pigeonholes children into fixed identities.

Our mechanism

Passion is measured by voluntary, unprompted return after novelty, praise, and deadlines fade (7- and 30-day return). Tracked as a revisable hypothesis with competing explanations and coverage gaps — and it separates preferred topic from preferred work mode.

Why it's safe & defensible

It develops durable interest rather than chasing scores, and never pigeonholes a child — no fixed "you are an X kid" label. Humane and rigorous.

Evidence

Grounded in the modern science of interest and motivation.

Hidi & Renninger — 4-phase interest Deci & Ryan — Self-Determination Theory O'Keefe / Dweck / Walton — interest is developed
GT100K06 / 08
Safety & brand · the guardrails

The rules that keep this safe for children — and for the brand.

GUARDRAIL 01

Humans decide everything consequential

Models only advise or shadow. Every grade, credential, and high-stakes call is made by a human. No automated accusations, no automated verdicts.

GUARDRAIL 02

Internal benchmark, external humility

"MIT-level readiness" is an internal benchmark only. All brand-facing language is "elite academic preparation." We never borrow an outside institution's name.

GUARDRAIL 03

No fixed-ability castes

No permanent ability rankings or "talent tiers." Every learner model — including interest — is a revisable hypothesis, never a label.

  • ✓Synthetic-only beta. No live child data yet — every mechanism is validated on synthetic data before any student touches it.
  • ✓Privacy is structural. COPPA/FERPA-aligned; crypto-shredding gives real, verifiable erasure — not "we deleted it, trust us."
