# PassionLab follows passionBrainlift where it conflicts with gtBrainlift

**Context:** `gtBrainlift` argues for specialize-early / burn-breadth / time-is-not-the-score; `passionBrainlift` argues interest is *built* (not found), spikes stay reversible and plural, and the trustworthy signal is voluntary return.
**Decision:** For the passion product, `passionBrainlift` governs the conflicts — spikes are reversible + plural and validated by voluntary return, never locked-in early single-domain commitment.
**Why:** It protects durable (harmonious) motivation and avoids the foreclosure/burnout the evidence ties to controlled, irreversible early specialization. The academic side still runs the gt-side dose via TimeBack; this decision scopes only the passion pipeline.
