# Leaner interest-measurement validity program

**Context:** With no ground-truth labels for years, the recommended fix was a standing construct-validity program (behavior × blinded expert comparative judgment × light self/family) plus a randomized-exploration reserve to prevent feedback loops.
**Decision:** Ship the leaner version — behavior + a light kid/family check-in, keeping only the two zero-cost safeguards (the model can say "not sure yet," and we bank longitudinal outcomes from day one); the sampled human cross-check and the randomized reserve are dropped.
**Why:** Minimal human load and fully-optimized probes were prioritized. Accepted risk: early mis-reads are harder to catch and there is some self-fulfilling-prophecy risk, watched via coverage-breadth + reopen-rate. See `hardening/measurement-validity.md`.
